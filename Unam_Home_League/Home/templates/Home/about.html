UFA is a football governing body that runs football activities at the unam main campus, it mainly facilitate the the campus football league which consists of 16 teams with players from from different institutions in the whole country.

The main purpose of the league is supposed to create players to feed the main campus team that participate in the national premier league. Actually the body(ufa) make sure this league runs smoothly every year,administration work are in order,games take place,right players are registered and winners at the end of the season are awarded
